ANTI-WASTE PROTOCOL

The fashion industry is broken. Too much production, too fast, and most of it ends up in landfills.

UNMARKED ARCHIVE exists as the antithesis to this model. We only produce what has already been desired.

The "On-Demand" Model

We use Kickstarter to reverse the process: First we sell, then we produce.

Pieces produced depend on the number of orders received, up to a maximum of 100 units.

Why Unmarked?

In a world saturated with logos screaming for attention, we chose silence.

UNMARKED means unbranded. Untethered from noise. Free from the need to prove anything.
